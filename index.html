About

The story behind
the builder

I'm a QA Manager at Cognizant, leading quality engineering on the UPS client account. Over 17 years across India and the US, I've built and managed QA teams, driven automation adoption, and made sure enterprise software actually works for the people who depend on it.

Originally from Andhra Pradesh — living in the United States since 2014 with my family. That dual identity shapes every product I build: tools for NRI families, Telugu speakers, working parents, and job seekers who deserve software built with them in mind.

In 2026 I started building AI products solo as a side hustle, using Groq, Vercel, and Next.js. What started as curiosity became 10 live, shipped apps. My next chapter is a full pivot into AI Quality Engineering, AI Product Management, or Prompt Engineering at a company where both mindsets matter.
